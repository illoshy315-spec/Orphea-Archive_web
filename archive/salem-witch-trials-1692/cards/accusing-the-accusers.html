Record 04 · Instance 01 — Massachusetts Bay, 1692

Calling out the liars got you hanged faster

“She’s obviously faking it” was the last logical thing some people ever said.

To a modern eye, the girls’ convulsions look staged. Several defendants said exactly that, out loud, in court.

It did not go well for them. Publicly accusing the afflicted girls of lying escalated a defendant from “suspected” to “confirmed” faster than almost anything else a person could do.
