Record 10 · Instance 02 — Campania, 79 AD

The mother holding the child was neither

For a century, visitors wept over a mother shielding her baby. In 2024, the DNA came back.

The adult with the golden bracelet, cradling a child on their lap: an adult male, genetically unrelated to the child. The two figures locked in a final embrace, displayed for decades as sisters, or mother and daughter: at least one of them male.

The casts preserved the dead exactly. The stories draped over them were ours.
