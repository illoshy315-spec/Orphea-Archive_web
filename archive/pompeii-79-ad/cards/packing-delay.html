Record 03 · Instance 02 — Campania, 79 AD

They died with their luggage

The houses of survivors were found empty. The dead were found holding things.

Excavators keep finding the same contrast: strongboxes emptied and carts missing from the homes of people who escaped early — and skeletons in the streets clutching coin purses, jewelry, silverware, keys.

Every hour spent gathering property was an hour of stone accumulating on the roads out. The pumice did not negotiate with net worth.
