Record 06 · Instance 02 — Campania, 79 AD

Some of the dead were on duty

The city had eighteen hours to empty. Not everyone in it was allowed to use them.

A Roman household slave could not simply leave: legally, they were property, and walking away made them a fugitive. When masters fled, someone was often told to stay and guard the house.

Researchers studying victims’ savings found many died carrying almost nothing — pointing to the city’s enslaved and poorest as the ones still inside when the surges came.
