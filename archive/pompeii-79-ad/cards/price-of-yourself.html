Record 11 · Instance 02 — Campania, 79 AD

Freedom was for sale — and your owner set the price

Perhaps a third of the city was property. The exit existed. It had a price tag.

Roman slavery came with a feature later slave systems deleted: a legal exit. A slave could hold savings — the peculium — and use it to buy their own freedom. Freed slaves became citizens; some of Pompeii’s grandest houses belonged to them.

Look closer at the arithmetic, though. The savings legally belonged to the owner. So did the price. Hope was part of the machinery.
