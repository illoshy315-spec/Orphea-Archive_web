About Orphea Archive

✦

I don't run this place to entertain you. The traps in these simulations aren't invented — every legal loophole, every courtroom procedure, every debated theory a player gets caught by is drawn from an actual record. Each instance in the Archive drops you into the worst moment history has on file — the Salem witch trials first, more to come — with no advantages beyond the ones history itself provided. This is where I keep the receipts.

I don't inflate my own confidence. Records are marked ✓ verified, ◐ verifying, or ⚠ contested depending on how solid the sourcing actually is. If I'm not sure, I say so.

Orphea Archive is researched and maintained by an independent creator. Found something wrong, missing a source, or worth adding? Write to orpheaarchive@gmail.com.
