About Orphea Archive

✦

Orphea Archive is the historical record division of Archive Oculus, a series of survival simulations set at the worst possible moments in history. Each simulation drops a player into a real historical crisis — the Salem witch trials, and more to come — with no advantages except the ones history itself provided.

This site exists because the traps inside those simulations aren't invented. Every mechanism a player can be caught by — a legal loophole, a courtroom procedure, a debated scientific theory — is drawn from a documented historical record. The Archive exists to show the paperwork: what's confirmed, what's still contested, and where it came from.

Orphea Archive is written and maintained by an independent creator, researching each simulation's history before it becomes a game. Facts are marked ✓ verified, ◐ verifying, or ⚠ contested depending on how solid the sourcing is — nothing is presented as more certain than it actually is.

Found a record that's wrong, missing a source, or worth adding? Write to orpheaarchive@gmail.com.
